Hi, I'm Celine

Front-end developer based in San Diego, California

From nurturing young minds as a dedicated nanny to stepping into the world of coding, I bring a diverse range of experiences and perspectives to the table. I recently completed a comprehensive bootcamp called SheCodes, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in web development and programming. While I cherish my time spent caring for children and helping them grow, my fascination with the digital realm has led me to explore the world of coding. My aspiration to create impactful and user-friendly online experiences drives me to continuously learn and improve my skills.

When I'm not immersed in the world of coding, you can find me pursuing my love for the outdoors, the beach is my happy place. I'm an avid reader who enjoys getting lost in the pages of a good book, and I often find myself exploring hiking trails and going camping to reconnect with nature.
My journey from being a nurturing nanny to a web developer is a testament to my adaptability and eagerness to embrace new challenges. I'm excited to blend my nurturing instincts with my newfound tech skills to create digital experiences that are intuitive, informative, and enjoyable.

Thank you for joining me on this transformative journey. Let's connect, collaborate, and create something wonderful together!
